Westlife Tribute Page!

Bio:

Westlife are an Irish pop vocal group formed in 1998 in Sligo, Ireland. They started with 5 members and performed ballads and pop songs that helped them become one of the most successful brands in Europe at the time. Westlife released six platinum-selling records and sold more than 40 million albums worldwide.

In 2004, one of the group members, Brian McFadden, left to pursue a solo career. The band continued to perform with 4 members until they officially disbanded in 2012.

In 2018, the band reunited with Shane Filan, Mark Feehily, Kian Egan, and Nicky Byrne, after numerous rumors. They announced that a new album will be soon released collaborating with Steve Mac, who is currently a successful songwriter with Ed Sheeran, a top pop star of the 2010s.
